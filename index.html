WELCOME

Thank you for visiting my personal webpage.
I am a Senior Assistant Professor in the Medical Institute of Developmental Disabilities Research, Showa University, Tokyo, Japan. My research goal is to understand the causes of difficulties for people with neurodevelopmental disorders using cognitive neuroscience, machine learning, and other methods.
Please don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or comments regarding my research projects. My e-mail address is as follows:

E-mail: ita3_at_med.showa-u.ac.jp

Research Interests

  • Neurodevelopmental conditions (autism spectrum disorder, attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder)
  • Neuroimaging (fMRI, DWI, Neuromelanin-sensitive MRI)
  • Machine learning
  • Subjective experiences
